Drinking-water risk classifier — preprocessing
=============================================
Label encoding : Safe=0, Moderate=1, High=2 (explicit ordinal map)
Leakage control: excluded bis_exceedances + BIS-tested chemistry (+ z-scores)
Kept features  : district (one-hot), year, na, k, co3, hco3 (+ z-scores)
Imbalance      : SMOTE (k_neighbors=5) on training split only
Model          : XGBoost (tuned via RandomizedSearchCV, f1_macro)
